JR Arts

Role

Web Design & Front-End Development

Technologies

Phonegap, Flickr API, HTML5, Css3, JavaScript, Photoshop

Objective

The design and development team at DG built a Hybrid Mobile Application for a talented artist to display her work in a gaming application fashion. Featuring the custom models and the design outfits for an authentic front row fashion experience, which allows players to play the Memory and Puzzles Game with a special characteristics and arts. Showing the Art skills was of course a focal point. Therefore the team produced a gallery fetched from a flickr account, which included an animated slider.
