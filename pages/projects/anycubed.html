Overview

Anycubed is a startup company for delivering goods/products which can be found in close proximity shops to the customers either by walkers, bikers or drivers. Goods will consist of items that can be found within 7-eleven shops for instance a sweet treat or a bottle of water, bread, chips, drinks, etc.

They chose DG to design and develop the entire project.

Objective

In an era filled with delivery applications, Anycubed wanted to stand out as the best delivery company that let the customers order Anything, Anywhere at Anytime in Hong Kong with the best user experience.

DG managed the project from Inception and scoping to stage environment. Our team developed 2 different set of applictions for their customers and drivers, each have a native mobile app (iOS & Android) and a Web app. As well as CMS system to manage their drivers, customers and products in real time, as well as graphical and UI design.
